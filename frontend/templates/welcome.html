The Problem

Sound familiar?

Students face the same pain points every semester. Aegis was built to solve exactly these.

No study plan

You know you should study, but figuring out when and what to study feels overwhelming before you even start.

Always catching up

You only realize you're lost after the lecture. By then, review takes twice as long and anxiety is already there.

Procrastination

Without a clear, manageable plan, every study session feels like climbing a mountain. So you delay until it's too late.

Information scattered

Timetables, deadlines, module notes, and resources live in five different places. Context-switching destroys focus.

The Process

How Aegis works

Set up once. Let Aegis handle the planning every week, automatically.

01

Upload your timetables

Drag and drop your class timetable and module files. Aegis accepts PDFs, spreadsheets, and more.

02

Aegis extracts & understands

Our AI reads your schedules, modules, and deadlines—building a complete picture of your academic life.

03

Get your weekly study plan

A personalised weekly plan is generated, balancing your commitments and study needs automatically.

04

Pre-class summaries & quizzes

Before each class, get an AI-generated brief so you walk in prepared. After class, reinforce with quizzes and flashcards.

05

Track habits & improve

Aegis tracks your study behaviour, surfaces insights, and nudges you toward better habits week after week.
