How the Tracker Works

Auto-Start & Auto-End

Sessions start and end automatically at the times set in your study plan. No manual action needed.

Study Time

Accumulates while you are active on the page (mouse, keyboard, or scroll detected).

Warning Popup (2 min)

Inactivity and distraction episodes each trigger one warning popup at the 2 minute mark.

Session Result

When the session ends, studying at least 70% of the planned time marks it completed. Below 70% is incompleted.
